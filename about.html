ABOUT WOODFOX

Fresh roasted coffee,
built around ritual, quality, and Houston roots.

Woodfox Roasters is a modern coffee brand focused on fresh roasting, clean design, and dependable supply for cafés, offices, and people who genuinely care about better coffee.

ABOUT US

Roasted with intention.

We believe coffee should feel elevated but approachable. Woodfox was built around the idea that great coffee can become part of someone’s daily rhythm — whether that’s a morning cup at home, a café program, or a company office keeping its team stocked with fresh beans.

WHAT WE DO

Small-batch roasting and supply.

We roast fresh coffee in Houston and provide curated offerings for wholesale partners, corporate offices, cafés, and private-label or collaborative opportunities. Our focus is on quality, consistency, and a clean brand experience from bag to brew.

OUR STORY

Modern coffee with local character.

Woodfox Roasters started with a simple vision: create a coffee brand that feels modern, memorable, and rooted in craftsmanship. We wanted something that could live naturally in both creative spaces and professional environments — a brand that looks sharp, tastes exceptional, and grows with the people who drink it.

WHY IT MATTERS

Freshness changes everything.

Fresh roasting means better aroma, better flavor, and a better overall coffee experience. From espresso to batch brew, we’re focused on delivering coffee that feels intentional and premium without losing warmth or simplicity.

WORK WITH US

Interested in wholesale, office coffee, or collaborations?

We’re building Woodfox for cafés, offices, creative spaces, and partners who want quality coffee with a strong visual identity.
